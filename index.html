Privacy Policy

US Civics Test 2025

Effective Date: February 23, 2026
Developer: Jorge Toledo (Toledo Advisory Services LLC)

Overview

US Civics Test 2025 ("the App") is a study tool for the United States naturalization civics test. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • No personal information is requested or stored.
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are collected.
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking technologies are used.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No cookies or similar tracking mechanisms are employed.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your study progress (which questions you have answered, your mastery level, and practice test results) locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party.
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the in-app "Reset Progress" feature.

Internet Access

The App does not require or use an internet connection. All content is bundled within the App itself. The only outbound link is a user-initiated link to the official USCIS website (uscis.gov) from the Settings screen.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by virtue of collecting no data whatsoever.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL and the effective date will be updated. Since the App collects no data, substantive changes are unlikely.
